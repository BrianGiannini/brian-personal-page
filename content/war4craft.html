War4craft: Battle Realms

February 2013

The mod was solo-developed by myself for StarCraft II during the Heart of the Swarm expansion, using 3D models extracted from World of Warcraft. As a huge fan of Battle Realms — especially its gameplay mechanics — I was heavily influenced by the game, hence the title. I released several gameplay videos throughout the development process, along with a launch video. The mod received some press coverage on various websites, as people were excited to see something that looked like a Warcraft 4 at the time.
